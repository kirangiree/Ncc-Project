Major. Gen. Raghu Bhandary (Retd)

National Cadet Corps

Nepal











Major General Raghu Bhandary (Retd) was born in Kathmandu, Nepal on 29 October 1963 and enrolled in the then Royal Nepalese Army on 1984 as an officer cadet. Major General Bhandary (Retd) commissioned in January 1986 as 2nd Lieutenant as an Infantry officer to The Famous Mahindra Dal Battalion.

During 36 years of service career he had successfully completed the various professional courses and proved his competency at various levels. Besides various trainings and seminars within the country, an alumnus of Army Command and Staff College, Shivapuri, Nepal and National Defense University (NDU), PLA, China, and has earned Master’s Degrees in Military Science, Science of National Security Strategy from National Defense University, China. Major General Bhandary (Retd) also acquired graduation degree from Tribhuvan University, Nepal in Humanities and Social Science in 1993.

During his service period Major General Bhandary (Retd) hold some of the important command, staff instructional appointments. He had commanded Infantry Company, Infantry Battalion, Chief Administrator of Medical Services Management Office, Brigade commander of No. 1 Brigade, Director of Directorate of Welfare and Planning. Major General Bhandary (Retd) has served as Master General of Ordnance (MGO) and Quarter Master General (QMG) simultaneously, Director General of Staff Duties, Planning and Policy (DGSDPP) and currently appointed as Director General of National Cadet Corps on 29 October 2020.

He has served in various United Nations Peacekeeping Operations that include UNIFIL, Lebanon in 1991 and 1994. He had served as Military Observer in UNMOP, Croatia in 2001 and as a Chief of Observer group Golan, Tiberias, Israel under UNDOF and UNTSO in 2010 responsible for maintenance of peace in Golan Height keeping close coordination with the authorities of Israel and Syria with more than 30 international officer under his command from throughout the world.

Major General Bhandary (Retd) is adorned with some of the prestigious medals and decorations such as "SUPRAWAL JANASEWA SHREE", "GORKHA DAKSHINBAHU-IV", "DAIBI PRAKOP UDDAR PADAK (OM)", "Foreign Service Medal", various UN Service Medals. And COAS Commendation Badge.

Major General Bhandary (Retd), finds pleasure in sightseeing and trekking as his favorite leisure pursuit. He amuses himself in keeping up with contemporary media and information technology. He also believes in gaining knowledge through self-study. He has already visited a number of countries namely Austria, Bosnia, China, Croatia, Egypt, England, France, Germany, India, Israel, Italy, Jordan, Lebanon, Pakistan, Serbia, Syria, Thailand and USA. Major General Bhandary (Retd) remains happy with his spouse Mrs Shanta Bhandary and the couple is blessed with a daughter Dr Rakshya Bhandary and a son Sakar Bhandary.
